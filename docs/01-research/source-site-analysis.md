## Project


## Purpose of This Document

This document summarizes the current Hanover Accountants & Advisors website, including its existing pages, visible content, services, positioning, audience signals, industry references, conversion paths, location information, and rebuild opportunities.

This document should be used as a factual foundation for the new site build.

Do not invent business facts beyond what is verified or approved by the client.

## Current Online Tax Preparation Process

The current tax preparation process includes four steps:

```txt
1. Schedule a consult
2. Upload documents
3. Prepare and review
4. E-sign and e-file
```

## Step 1: Schedule a Consult

The site says experts conduct an initial review of the current tax situation by phone or virtual meeting.

## Step 2: Upload Documents

The site says the client receives an email invitation with a secured portal to upload tax documents and complete a tax questionnaire.

## Step 3: Prepare and Review

The site says tax preparation is completed within 1 to 3 weeks, followed by a professional review session through the portal.

## Step 4: E-sign and E-file

The site says clients receive a secured email to complete authorized e-signature, after which the return is e-filed and confirmed received by the IRS.
