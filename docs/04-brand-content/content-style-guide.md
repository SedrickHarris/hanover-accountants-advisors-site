# Content Style Guide

## Project

Hanover Accountants & Advisors Website Rebuild

## Purpose of This Document

This document defines the writing style, content rules, formatting standards, trust guidelines, SEO/AEO approach, local page requirements, and conversion copy standards for the Hanover Accountants & Advisors website rebuild.

The site must communicate clearly, professionally, and accurately while supporting:

* SEO
* GEO
* AEO
* Nationwide service visibility
* Local service-area visibility
* Audience-specific search intent
* LLM search visibility
* Featured snippets
* People Also Ask
* Rich snippets
* Trust
* Customer engagement
* Customer conversion

The content should be useful, specific, human-first, and trustworthy.

---

# 1. Brand Voice

The Hanover voice should be:

```txt
Professional
Clear
Helpful
Trustworthy
Plain-English
Practical
Calm
Accurate
Client-focused
Organized
Confident without hype
```

The site should feel like a reliable accounting and advisory partner, not a loud marketing site.

## Voice Examples

Good:

```txt
Hanover helps businesses keep financial records organized, prepare for tax deadlines, and make more informed decisions through secure online accounting support.
```

Avoid:

```txt
We crush your taxes, dominate your bookkeeping, and guarantee your business will grow faster than ever.
```

# 3. Public-Facing Copy Rules

## Use Plain English

Write for business owners, entrepreneurs, individuals, and decision-makers who may not know accounting terminology.

Good:

```txt
Cash flow management helps a business understand when money is coming in, when money is going out, and whether there is enough cash to cover upcoming needs.
```

Avoid:

```txt
Cash flow management optimizes liquidity timing across operational receivables, disbursement cycles, and working capital exposure.
```

## Explain Jargon

If an accounting, tax, or advisory term is necessary, explain it.

Example:

```txt
Client accounting services, often called CAS, are recurring accounting services that help a business keep books, reports, and financial records organized throughout the year.
```

---

# 4. No Em Dash Rule

Avoid em dashes in public-facing copy.

Do not use:

```txt
—
```

Use instead:

```txt
Commas
Periods
Colons
Semicolons
Parentheses
Shorter sentences
```

Bad:

```txt
Hanover helps businesses stay organized — from bookkeeping to tax preparation.
```

Good:

```txt
Hanover helps businesses stay organized, from bookkeeping to tax preparation.
```

---

# 5. Accuracy and Compliance Rules

The site must not provide personalized legal, tax, or financial advice.

Use educational and service-oriented language.

## Safe Language

```txt
Tax treatment depends on your specific situation.
A consultation can help determine what support may be appropriate.
Hanover can help clients review records, organize information, and prepare for tax and accounting needs.
```

## Avoid

```txt
You will qualify for this deduction.
This strategy will lower your taxes.
Hanover guarantees compliance.
Hanover guarantees the maximum refund.
```

---

Do not invent:

```txt
Credentials

Certifications

Reviews

Client results

Packages
Office addresses
Business hours
Physical offices
Guarantees

Software names

```

If a claim is not verified, do not publish it as fact.

```txt
Client to confirm.
Needs confirmation.
Use general language until verified.
```

# 9. Richmond and Alexandria Language Rule

The existing site has a Virginia location inconsistency.

Until confirmed, avoid stating that Richmond is a physical office.

## Safe Language

```txt
Hanover serves clients in Richmond, VA through secure online accounting, tax, bookkeeping, payroll, and advisory workflows.
```

```txt
Hanover supports clients in Alexandria, VA with accounting, tax, bookkeeping, payroll, and advisory services.
```

## Avoid Until Confirmed

```txt
Hanover's Richmond office is located in Alexandria.
Visit Hanover's Richmond office.
Hanover has a physical office in Richmond.
```

# 15. FAQ Writing Standards

FAQs should support AEO, PAA, featured snippets, and user clarity.

## FAQ Answer Length

Most FAQ answers should be:

```txt
40 to 90 words
```

## FAQ Answer Formula

```txt
Answer the question directly.
Add one short clarifying detail.
Guide the user to the next step if relevant.
```

## Good FAQ Example

Question:

```txt
Can Hanover provide bookkeeping services online?
```

Answer:

```txt
Yes. Hanover provides online bookkeeping services through secure workflows that allow clients to share documents, organize records, and receive professional support remotely. A consultation can help determine what level of bookkeeping support fits the client's needs.
```

## Bad FAQ Example

```txt
Yes, we are the best online bookkeeping company and guarantee better books fast.
```

# 16. Direct Answer Section Standards

Every major service, audience, nationwide, local, and matrix page should include a direct answer near the top.

## Purpose

Direct answer sections support:

```txt
AEO
Featured snippets
People Also Ask
LLM extraction
User clarity
```

## Direct Answer Style

Use 2 to 4 sentences.

Avoid fluff.

Example:

```txt
Hanover provides payroll services for small businesses through secure online workflows and professional support. The service can help businesses manage payroll processing, payroll tax filing support, and employee earnings statement needs with clearer organization and fewer manual steps.
```

# 18. Formatting Standards

Use:

```txt
Short paragraphs
Clear H2s and H3s
Bullets for scannability
Tables only when helpful
Direct answers
FAQ sections
CTA blocks
Related links
```

Avoid:

```txt
Long unbroken paragraphs
Overuse of bold text
Keyword-stuffed headings
Multiple H1s
Inconsistent capitalization
```

## Paragraph Length

Most paragraphs should be:

```txt
1 to 4 sentences
```

## Sentence Style

Use a mix of sentence lengths, but keep copy easy to scan.

# 19. Heading Rules

Every page should have one H1.

Use H2s for major sections.

Use H3s for subsections.

## Example Service Page Heading Flow

```txt
H1: Online Bookkeeping Services
H2: Online Bookkeeping That Helps Keep Your Records Organized
H2: What Online Bookkeeping Includes
H2: Who This Service Helps
H2: How Hanover's Online Bookkeeping Process Works
H2: Related Accounting Services
H2: Online Bookkeeping FAQs
H2: Schedule a Consultation
```

## Avoid

```txt
Multiple H1s
Headings that are just keywords
Repeated identical H2s across every page
```

# 20. Keyword Use Rules

Keywords should be used naturally.

Use primary terms in:

```txt
Title tag
H1
First paragraph
Direct answer
One or two H2s
FAQ questions
Internal anchor text
```

Do not force keywords into every sentence.

## Good

```txt
Hanover provides online bookkeeping services for businesses that need organized records, clearer reporting, and secure professional support.
```

## Bad

```txt
Our online bookkeeping services are the best online bookkeeping services for online bookkeeping services nationwide.
```
