## Project Purpose

This project is a full strategic website rebuild for Hanover Accountants & Advisors.

The new website will reposition Hanover as a nationwide online accounting, bookkeeping, tax preparation, payroll, outsourced CFO, and advisory firm with strong local service-area support for Georgia and Virginia markets.

The goal is to create a modern, scalable website that supports national visibility, local visibility, audience-specific search intent, topical authority, trust, and customer conversion.

## Primary Positioning

Hanover Accountants & Advisors provides nationwide online accounting, bookkeeping, tax preparation, payroll, outsourced CFO, and advisory services through secure technology and dedicated professionals.

The site should communicate that Hanover can serve clients across the United States while also maintaining local relevance in Atlanta, Georgia, nearby Georgia markets, Alexandria, Virginia, nearby Virginia markets, and Richmond, Virginia if confirmed.

## Service Area Strategy

The website will use a nationwide-first service-area structure.

The service-area strategy has three levels:

```txt
Nationwide service availability
Confirmed office or primary local markets
Nearby local service-area markets
```

The site should not be built as only a local accounting firm site. Hanover's national online service model should be clear across the homepage, service pages, audience pages, and nationwide service-area pages.

## Location Accuracy Note

The existing site includes Virginia office language that needs clarification.

Before final launch, confirm:

```txt
Is Alexandria, Virginia a physical office?
Is Richmond, Virginia a physical office?
Is Richmond, Virginia only a service area?
Is the Richmond language old or incorrect copy?
Which Virginia address should be used publicly?
Which locations should be connected to Google Business Profile or Apple Business Connect?
```

Until confirmed, use careful language:

```txt
Serving clients in [market]
Online accounting services available in [market]
Service-area support for [market]
Virtual consultations available for clients in [market]
```

Do not use physical office language unless the office is verified.
